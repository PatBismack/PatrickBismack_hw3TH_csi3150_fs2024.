Cities, Beaches & Moutains from San Francisco

Your Cities, Beaches, & Moutains adventure starts with a sceanic drive to Muir Woods. The bus departs from the pickup spot located at 333 O'Farrell St at 7:15am. After traveling across the golden gate bridge, you will descend into the Muir Woods Redwood Grove.

What To Wear

Your trip includes professional guides, water, and a small snack. Dress warmly in layers as conditions around northern california can change quickly. Please bring a small bag, warm clothes(wear synthetic layers and bring a wind breaker), sunglasses and water bottle. You may wish to bring binoculars and a camera if you have them.
